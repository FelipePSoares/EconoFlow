Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to our financial control application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

2. Collected Information

2.1 Personal Data

We collect the following information when you register and use our application:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Financial data (transactions, income, expenses)
  • Login and authentication information

2.2 Automatic Data

We may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Browsing information
  • Application usage logs

3. Information Use

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize our services
  • Send service-related communications
  • Improve user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Internal Sharing

Your information is accessed only by internal teams strictly necessary for service operation.

4.2 External Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When legally required
  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption
  • Access controls
  • System monitoring
  • Secure backups
  • Protection against unauthorized access

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct incorrect information
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Revoke consents

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Improve navigation
  • Personalize experience
  • Analyze platform usage

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. We recommend regular reviews.

9. Contact and Legislation

9.1 Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: contact@econoflow.pt

9.2 Applicable Legislation

This policy follows the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
